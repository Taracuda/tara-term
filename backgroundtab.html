A little backstory...

Personal Background

I was born and grew up in Florence, AL with my parents, sister, and brother. I graduated from high school and received a scholarship to perform as a majorette in the marching band at the University of North Alabama. I performed in the band for 3 years, then completed my Bachelor Degree in nursing. My chosen specialty was adult cardiac care.

Here's the UNA halftime performance my last year on the line.

Beginning of Nursing Career to Now

I spent 6 years working as a registered nurse, primarily in Birmingham, AL. My specialty remained adult cardiac at my "home hospital", but I worked in medical-surgical as well as spinal cord rehabilitation during a year period of time as a travel nurse. During my nursing career, I got married to my husband Robert and we settled into our new home. Although I loved many aspects of my nursing career, I eventually came to a point of being so emotionally and physically burned out and I made the difficult decision to leave the career I knew and embark on a new path - software development!

I took a leap of faith, after lots of reading and research, and applied to the Master of Science in Computer Information Systems program at Boston University. I was accepted into the program in 2019, and will be completing my coursework for a Masters in CIS with a concentration in web application development in December 2021. I've learned so much since beginning at BU, and I have spent the last year getting hands-on experience as an intern software developer for a large manufacturing company here in Birmingham. I will be taking a full-time position as a software developer there in December.
